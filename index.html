Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026

PanelCut PRO respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your information.

1. Information Collection

PanelCut PRO does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The application does not require user registration and does not request personal data such as names, email addresses, phone numbers, or payment information.

2. Local Data Storage

All projects, settings, and files created or managed within PanelCut PRO are stored locally on the user's device or in a location chosen by the user.

The developer does not have access to these files or data.

3. Data Sharing

PanelCut PRO does not sell, share, rent, or disclose user data to third parties.

4. Device Permissions

The application may request access to device storage solely for the purpose of opening, saving, importing, and exporting project files and reports as requested by the user.

Files accessed by the application remain under the user's control and are not transmitted outside the device.

5. Internet Connectivity

At the time of publication, PanelCut PRO does not use internet connectivity to collect user information.

Should future versions introduce cloud services, analytics, synchronization features, or other online functionality, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Security

Reasonable efforts are made to ensure the reliability and security of the application. However, users are responsible for maintaining backups of their own projects and data.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the latest version available through the application or its distribution platform.

8. Contact Information

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer through the application's Google Play listing.
